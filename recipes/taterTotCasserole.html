Tater Tot Casserole

Description

This tater tot casserole recipe is easy to make and tastes great!

Ingredients

  • Tater Tots
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, onion, and garlic. Cook and stir until beef is browned and crumbly, about 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and discard grease. Stir in tater tots, tomato sauce, and seasonings.
  4. Transfer to a large baking dish. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and stir. Bake until heated through, about 10 minutes more.
